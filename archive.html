About Us

MCNITY Official Website

We are Minecraft Server from Indonesia. Established on April 2022. We are one of the minecraft servers that can be played in both editions, namely Bedrock edition and Java edition.

Our server is dominated by Survival Economy mode, where players interact with each other, sell or buy goods with currency on the server, then survive like normal survival.
