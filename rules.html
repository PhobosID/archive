Server Rules

For the enjoyment of all players, our staff team work hard to provide a good and enjoyable environment. We have a few rules and guidelines that all members must adhere to in order to ensure everyone has fun.

Survival Rules

The Rules Punishment
Don't teleport all players to you Permanent Ban
Griefing and stealing items are not allowed 30 Days Ban, Reset Money & Inventory
Do not Teleport other players to yourself and kill them (TP-Kill) or vice versa 7 Days Ban, Reset money, Delete Faction & /tp command deleted from your account
Do not trap and then kill other players (example: You tell another player to go to "/pwarp x" and then when they teleport, you kill them) 7 Days Ban, Reset money, Delete Faction & the corresponding command is deleted from your account
GameBoost (GB) is not allowed 7 Day Ban, Reset money, Delete Faction
Do not use /nick (other player/staff) without permission Ban 3 Days & remove command /nick from your account
Do not hit players from the safezone (Arena / world that pvp on) Warn-Take items-Take money-Reset money-Ban
Do not spam /say or /me on the server Warn-Kick-Money reset & remove command /say or /me from your account
Do not spam or abuse /size Warn-Kick-Money reset & remove /size command from your account
Faction is forbidden to have a leader whose rank is below Phoenix Forcedelete Faction
Do not build farm in Plot world & Near spawn in Survival world Your plot will be deleted

Free-For-All Rules

The Rules Punishment
Do not Teaming in FFA 30 Days Ban, Account reset
GameBoost (GB) is not allowed 30 Days Ban, Account reset

OneBlock Rules

The Rules Punishment
Do not use auto clicker on OneBlock servers 7 Days Ban, Island Reset, Money & Inventory reset
Do not AFK inside Oneblock server (Our staff will check). If you insist to afk type /transfer lobby first 3-Day Kick-Ban
Kill all mobs inside your island immediately is a must Warn-Kick-Ban 3 Days

Keep in mind that this rules applies to everyone, Even Intern and Staff. We appreciate your assistance in ensuring that the server is fun and secure for all users.
